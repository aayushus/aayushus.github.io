Chapter 02 · The Pipeline
The whole pipeline, in one diagram
Everything on one page: how a Jira Story flows through the agents to a shipped release, the Jira ticket lifecycle the agents key off, and the autonomous defect loop that closes bugs without a human in the path for 2 - HIGH / 3 - MEDIUM issues.

Nodes are colored by who owns them — the seven named agents, the humans, and the Atlassian artifacts they read from and write back to. Source of truth is Jira + Confluence in the JAGGAER instance; the same statuses, priorities, and labels used everywhere else in this playbook appear here verbatim.

Confluence spaces. The context chain reads from Confluence as well as Jira: PSF is the Product Management space (PRDs), IAA is the Infrastructure & Architecture space (ADRs), and DEVKB is the Development Knowledgebase (engineering patterns) — all in the JAGGAER Confluence at jira-jaggaer.atlassian.net/wiki.
The states agents key off
State machineJira ticket lifecycle
Agents read and write Jira status. The Ready for Work gate is the single rule that stops Coder or Mender from acting on a half-written ticket. Humans own Open, Resolved / Closed, and unblocking On Hold.
